Who We Are

Nasru Diin, formerly known as Dugsi Cloud was founded by Duraan Ali in 2016 after spending years in Islamic schools and seeing how unorganized student information was, he started developing Alif Cloud and officially launched it January 2017.

Our Mission

Allowing every Islamic school to use technology to thrive.

Modernizing old systems of education to catch up with the new world. Giving school managers, stuff and principals an advantage over other schools
